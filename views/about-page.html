Sylvester Stallone

Full Name: Michael Sylvester Gardenzio Stallone

Birth Date: July 6, 1946

Birth Place: New York City, New York, USA

Occupation: Actor, Director, Screenwriter

Sylvester Stallone is best known for his iconic role as Rocky Balboa in the "Rocky" film series, as well as for his portrayal of John Rambo in the "Rambo" film series. He is a talented actor, director, and screenwriter and has been entertaining audiences for decades.
